# Mean

The mean is the average value of a dataset.

## Formula

\[
\mu = \frac{\sum x}{N}
\]

Where:

- \(\sum x\) = Sum of all observations
- \(N\) = Number of observations
- \(\mu\) = Population Mean

For sample data:

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{\sum x}{n}
\]

Where:

- \(\bar{x}\) = Sample Mean
- \(n\) = Sample Size

---

# Example 1: Mean of Ungrouped Data

### Data

| x |
|---|
| 30 |
| 32 |
| 32 |
| 37 |
| 42 |
| 50 |
| 50 |

### Calculation

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{30 + 32 + 32 + 37 + 42 + 50 + 50}{7}
\]

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{273}{7}
\]

\[
\bar{x} = 39
\]

### Answer

**Mean = 39**

---

# Example 2: Mean Using Frequency Distribution

### Data

| Age (x) | Frequency (f) | f × x |
|----------|----------|----------|
| 14 | 76 | 1064 |
| 15 | 121 | 1815 |
| 16 | 118 | 1888 |
| 17 | 101 | 1717 |
| 18 | 54 | 972 |

### Formula

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{\sum fx}{\sum f}
\]

### Calculation

\[
\sum fx = 1064 + 1815 + 1888 + 1717 + 972
\]

\[
\sum fx = 7456
\]

\[
\sum f = 76 + 121 + 118 + 101 + 54
\]

\[
\sum f = 470
\]

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{7456}{470}
\]

\[
\bar{x} \approx 15.9
\]

### Answer

**Mean Age ≈ 15.9 years**

# Example 3: Mean of Grouped Data

### Weight Distribution

| Class Interval | Frequency (f) | Midpoint (x) |
|----------------|--------------|-------------|
| 4 – 6 | 3 | 5 |
| 7 – 8 | 11 | 7.5 |
| 9 – 10 | 14 | 9.5 |
| 10 – 15 | 6 | 12.5 |

### Formula

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{\sum fx}{\sum f}
\]

### Calculation

\[
\sum fx =
3(5) + 11(7.5) + 14(9.5) + 6(12.5)
\]

\[
= 15 + 82.5 + 133 + 75
\]

\[
= 305.5
\]

\[
\sum f = 3 + 11 + 14 + 6 = 34
\]

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{305.5}{34}
\]

\[
\bar{x} \approx 8.98
\]

### Answer

**Mean Weight ≈ 8.98 kg**

# Example 4: Mean Rainfall

### Data

| Rainfall (cm) | Frequency (f) | Midpoint (x) |
|--------------|--------------|-------------|
| 0 – 2 | 96 | 1 |
| 2 – 5 | 18 | 3.5 |
| 5 – 10 | 7 | 7.5 |
| 10 – 30 | 4 | 20 |
| 30+ | 0 | - |

### Calculation

\[
\sum fx =
96(1) + 18(3.5) + 7(7.5) + 4(20)
\]

\[
= 96 + 63 + 52.5 + 80
\]

\[
= 291.5
\]

\[
\sum f = 96 + 18 + 7 + 4 = 125
\]

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{291.5}{125}
\]

\[
\bar{x} \approx 2.33
\]

### Answer

**Average Rainfall ≈ 2.33 cm**

# Quick Summary

## Mean

\[
\text{Mean} = \frac{\text{Sum of Observations}}{\text{Number of Observations}}
\]

### Ungrouped Data

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{\sum x}{n}
\]

### Frequency Distribution

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{\sum fx}{\sum f}
\]

### Grouped Data

1. Find class midpoints.
2. Compute \(f \times x\).
3. Calculate \(\sum fx\).
4. Calculate \(\sum f\).
5. Use:

\[
\bar{x} = \frac{\sum fx}{\sum f}
\]
